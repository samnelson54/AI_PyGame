How to Play
In my game your objective is to survive the waves of enemy cubes and achieve a score of 100 to win. Use the WASD keys to move your cube and the mouse to aim and shoot at enemies. Click to fire the gun. Avoid enemy cubes to not die

Peer Review Feedback
i got suggestions to make the game easier, Based on their feedback, I reduced the number of incoming enemies to balance the gameplay and make it more enjoyable.
